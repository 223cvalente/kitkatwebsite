About Kit Kats


The Kit Kat candy bar dates back to 1935 when it was launched in England. Originally, it was known as “Rowntree's Chocolate Crisp,” but in 1937 the candy bar was renamed as "Kit Kat Chocolate Crisp" and eventually became just Kit Kat. What makes KitKat special? Kit Kat is the world's first chocolate bar made from 100% sustainable cocoa. Kit Kat has a case to be the healthiest candy bar in the market (because it's mostly wafer and air).
